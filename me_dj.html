Me as a DJ

I really enjoy listening to music and am an occasional DJ

On this page, I'd like to delve more into my hobbies and interests. One of them is music. I absolutely adore listening to music, whether it's at the gym, in the car, or while coding.
My favorite music genres include Techno, German rap, and music with a Depeche Mode vibe. I've inherited my taste directly from my dad. He's always had cars with great sound systems. Our last Golf even had an SD card slot.
Dad doesn't use Spotify (he only pays for it); instead, he still has a lot of old MP3s and CDs that he enjoys. He could always tell me something interesting about each song.
My all-time favorite story is about "Owner of a Lonely Heart," where he'd tell me that he drove past the White House with this song playing during his time studying in the USA.
After that meandering digression, I'd like to now turn to my other interests...

Of course, I've also been into gaming. It used to be a more significant part of my life than it is today.

I was really good in Minecraft, and when the COVID-19 crisis hit in 2020, and all my friends who previously had PlayStations got PCs, it naturally opened up the opportunity for us to play together.

Initially, we played on public Minecraft servers until we eventually decided to create our own Minecraft server.

I had tried setting up a server a few times before with my best friend, but it failed because of LAN port issues. However, we eventually got it to work, and I could establish a Minecraft world for my friends.

I also installed plugins on the server, and so on. However, that was the first time I really delved deep into working with computers.

I've been involved with video and image editing from an early age. I started with paint.net and Shotcut, and now I use Adobe Premiere Pro and Shotcut.

You can watch some of my videos on the HOMEPAGE and on my YOUTUBE CHANNEL, where I created YouTube tutorials for my school during the 9th and 10th grades, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.

As the COVID-19 pandemic was winding down, I delved into Blender and naturally got into a lot of node-based programming.

Initially, I wanted to become a 3D artist because, unlike video editing, I could listen to music while working, which is crucial for me. However, as I got more into actively coding, I found it to be even cooler.

Nevertheless, Blender has been a significant asset for me, and I'm proud of my foundational knowledge.
