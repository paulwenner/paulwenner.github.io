Me as a an athlete

I have been actively doing strength training for two years now

Since November 2021, I've been actively engaging in strength training, hitting the gym 3-6 times a week. I consider a healthy and strong body to be important, and as a student, I have the time to make it a priority.
However, I have a good older friend named Markus who doesn't visit the gym as often. Markus is an computer scientist, and I got to know him through another friend about a year ago. He shared a lot about the field of computer science with me.
In fact, he inspired me to start exploring computer science even before university. So, the gym has also helped me kickstart my journey into computer science, including starting with CS50.
